Know the Essentials

Before

Prepare an emergency kit, save contacts, and know your evacuation route.

During

Move to higher ground, stay away from floodwater, and follow alerts.

After

Return only when authorities say it is safe and avoid contaminated water.

⚠ Always follow local authorities’ instructions first.
