Tsunami Safety Guide

Click a section below to see what to do before, during, and after a tsunami.

  • Prepare an emergency kit
  • Save important contacts
  • Know your evacuation route
  • Move to higher ground
  • Avoid floodwater
  • Follow emergency alerts
  • Return only when safe
  • Check for damage
  • Avoid contaminated water

⚠️ Always follow local authorities’ instructions first.
